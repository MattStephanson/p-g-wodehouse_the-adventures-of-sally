Dedication Tο George Grossmith

Dear George⁠,—

The production of our mutual effort, The Cabaret Girl, is a week distant as I write this; and who shall say what the harvest will be? But, whether a week from now we are slapping each other on the back or shivering in the frost, nothing can alter the fact that we had a lot of fun writing the thing together. Not a reproach or a nasty look from start to finish. Because of this, and because you and I were side by side through the Adventure of the Ship’s Bore, the Episode of the Concert In Aid of the Seamen’s Orphans and Widows, and the Sinister Affair of The Rose of Stamboul, I dedicate this book to you.

P. G. Wodehouse.

Garrick Club.
